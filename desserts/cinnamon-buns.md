# Cinnamon buns

## Ingredients
flour
sugar
fresh yeast
butter
milk
salt
cardamom
cinnamon
sugar
butter
egg
pearl sugar

## Instructions
crush cardamom
disolve fresh yeast in milk
add sugar, most of the flour and salt
knead
knead in the butter
knead a little more (about 5 minutes)
let rest until doubled in volume
roll out into a rectangle, cover with butter, sugar and cinnamon
roll together to a roll
cut into pieces and place in paper moulds
let rest for approx 0,5-1h
cover with eggwash and pearl sugar
