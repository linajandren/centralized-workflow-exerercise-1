# Amazing salad

## Ingredients

- 20 small tomatos (preferably with different colours)
- 1 cucumber
- 1 pot of salad
- 1 tbs red wine vinegar
- 3 tbs
- salt
- bblack pepper

## Instructions

- Cut the tomatoes in half. Cut the cucumber into cubes. Trim and tear 
the lettuce into coarse pieces. Mix the tomatoes, cucumber and lettuce. 
Place in a bowl.

 - Mix vinegar and oil. Season with salt and pepper. Drizzle the 
dressing over the salad.
