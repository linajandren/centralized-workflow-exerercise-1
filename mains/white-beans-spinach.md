# White Beans with Spinach and Basil

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup raw cashews
- 2 cups vegetable broth
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1/2 small sweet onion diced small
- 4 tablespoons tomato paste
- (2) 15-ounce cans cannellini beans
- 4 cups baby spinach sliced
- 1/2 cup fresh basil sliced or chopped

## Instructions

1. Prepare the Cashew Cream: Soak cashews, drain, add vegetable broth and blend.

2. Warm the olive oil, add the tomato paste.

3. Stir in other ingredients. Serve with bread, pasta or rice.
