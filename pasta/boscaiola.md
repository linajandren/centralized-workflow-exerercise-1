# Penne boscaiola recipe


## Ingredients

- penne
- pancetta
- peas
- mushrooms
- onions


## Instructions

- Combine mushrooms in hot wine and let it stay for 10 minutes
- Lift mushrooms from the wine and squeeze them
- Cook the pasta in boiling water until *al dente*
- Stir fry mushrooms, peas and onions in a pan
- Combine it the veggies and the pasta
