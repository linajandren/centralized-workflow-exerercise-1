# Salsa Recipe

## Ingredients

- 4 ripe tomatoes
- 1/2 red onion
- 1 jalapeño pepper
- 1 clove garlic
- 1/4 cup cilantro
- 1 lime
- Salt to taste

## Instructions

- Dice tomatoes, onion, and jalapeño.
- Mince garlic and chop cilantro.
- Combine all ingredients in a bowl.
- Squeeze lime over mixture and add salt.
- Mix well and serve.
